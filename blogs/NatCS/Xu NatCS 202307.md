## [Xu, Y., Olmos, L.E., Mateo, D. et al. Urban dynamics through the lens of human mobility. Nat Comput Sci](https://doi.org/10.1038/s43588-023-00484-5)

### **Objective:**
- Explore a country's capacity for environmental mitigation, examining how various factors combined can potentially achieve desired goals such as net-zero emissions by 2050

### **Case:**
- 21 Cities in America, Asia and Europe; 11 Cities in Spain

### **Methodology:**
- Indicator
- SHAP

### **Data Source**
- Mobile phone
- Travel survey
- Lanscan

### **Findings:**

- Cities display socieconomic segregation patterns, independent of urban structure
