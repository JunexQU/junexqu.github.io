## [Hultman, N.E., Clarke, L., Frisch, C. et al. Fusing subnational with national climate action is central to decarbonization the case of the United States. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-020-18903-w)

### **Objective:**
- How a significant subset of those actors, including states, cities and businesses, are implementing policies that will significantly reduce economy-wide emissions

### **Case:**
- US

### **Methodology:**

### **Data Source**
