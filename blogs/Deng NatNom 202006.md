## [Deng, F., Lv, Z., Qi, L. et al. A big data approach to improving the vehicle emission inventory in China. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-020-16579-w)

### **Objective:**
- The total uncertainty regarding the heavy-duty truck movements continues to be different to evaluate

### **Case:**
- Jing-Jin-Ji, China

### **Methodology:**
- TrackATruck model

### **Data Source**
- Truck GPS: SINOIOV platform

### **Findings:**

- The HDT emissions were highly concentrated on a small number of intercity roads throughout the JJJ region
