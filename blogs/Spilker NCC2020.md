## [Spilker, G., Nguyen, Q., Koubi, V. et al. Attitudes of urban residents towards environmental migration in Kenya and Vietnam. Nat. Clim. Chang. 10, 622–627 (2020)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-020-0805-1)

### **Objective:**
- Existing studies overlook internal environmental migration

### **Case:**
- Kenya and Vietnam

### **Methodology:**
- Regression

### **Data Source**
- Survey

### **Findings:**

- Although residents in receiving areas view short-term climate events and long-term climate conditions as legitimate reasons to migrate, they do not see environmental migrants as more deserving than economic migrants.
