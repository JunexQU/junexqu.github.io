## [Wang, XT., Liu, H., Lv, ZF. et al. Trade-linked shipping CO2 emissions. Nat. Clim. Chang. 11, 945–951 (2021)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-021-01176-6)

### **Objective:**
- Establish a fine-scale linkage between shipping emissions and maritime trgade, as well as the drivers behind shipping emissions

### **Case:**
- Global countries, Grid

### **Methodology:**
- Energy efficiency operational indicator (EEOI)
- Voyage-based EEOI
- Minimize emission

### **Data Source: Open**
- Ship GPS: AIS and STSD, need license
- Trade data:
  - BACI
  - UN comtrade database
  - US census
  - Eurostat

### **Findings:**

- Dense shipping emissions on the circum-equatorial corridor
