## [Reia, S.M., Rao, P.S.C., Barthelemy, M. et al. Spatial structure of city population growth. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-33527-y)

### **Objective:**
- Study the spatial heterogeneity in city population growth by conduting an analysis of the most recent ACS county-to-county migration flows

### **Case:**
- US

### **Methodology:**
- OLS

### **Data Source**
- ACS

### **Findings:**

- 45.6 million people migrated to the US; 25.7 million people per year migrated within the same county
- 10.4 people moved between different counties within the same state; intra-state flows account for 24% of the domestic migration
