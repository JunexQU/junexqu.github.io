## [Anguelovski, I., Connolly, J.J.T., Cole, H. et al. Green gentrification in European and North American cities. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-31572-1)

### **Objective:**
- Test the hypothesis of green gentrification

### **Case:**
- West Europe and North American

### **Methodology:**
- Bayesian linear mixed model

### **Data Source**
- greenspaces
- population
- gentrification

### **Findings:**

- A strong relevant positive relationship between greenspaces inaugurated during 1990-2009, and recently emerging gentrification trends during 2010-2016
