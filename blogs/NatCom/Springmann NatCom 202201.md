## [Springmann, M., Freund, F. Options for reforming agricultural subsidies from health, climate, and economic perspectives. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-27645-2)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse options for reforming agricultural subsidies that are in line with health and climate-change objectives

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- CGE
- Agricultural detail: MAGNET

### **Data Source**
- Subsidy: OECD
- GTAP
- CO2

### **Findings:**

- Removing all agricultural subsidies by 2030 led to reduced production of crop, while regions without subsidies to remove reacted by increasing production

- The changes in consumption followed the changes in production, but were mediated by changes in trade and commodity prices

- The increases in mortality affected the labour supply and led to a reduction in economic welfare
