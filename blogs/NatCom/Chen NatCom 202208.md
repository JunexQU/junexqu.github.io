## [Chen, B., Wu, S., Song, Y. et al. Contrasting inequality in human exposure to greenspace between cities of Global North and Global South. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32258-4)

### **Objective:**
- Uncover the differences in human exposure to greenspace 

### **Case:**
- Global countries, cities

### **Methodology:**
- population-weighted-human exposure to greenspace
- Gini

### **Data Source**
- Administrative boundary: GAUL
- WorldPop
- World Cover

### **Findings:**

- 45% and 21% of countries and regions have human exposure to greenspace index values of less than 50% and 25%, respectively
