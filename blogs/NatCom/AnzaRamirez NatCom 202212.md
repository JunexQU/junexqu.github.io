## [Anza-Ramirez, C., Lazo, M., Zafra-Tanaka, J.H. et al. The urban built environment and adult BMI, obesity, and diabetes in Latin American cities. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-35648-w)

### **Objective:**
- Study the association between body mass index (BMI), obesity and type 2 diabetes (T2D) with built environment factors

### **Case:**
- Latin American cities

### **Methodology:**
- Regression

### **Data Source**
- 93,280 surveys
- NDVI

### **Findings:**

- Greenness is inversely associated with BMI and obesity
