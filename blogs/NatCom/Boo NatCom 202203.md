## [Boo, G., Darin, E., Leasure, D.R. et al. High-resolution population estimation using household survey data and building footprints. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-29094-x)

### **Objective:**
- Develop a Bayesian hierarchical model for bottom-up population estimation that leverages household surveys and building footprints

### **Case:**x
- Kinshasa

### **Methodology:**
- Regression with MCMC

### **Data Source**
- Household survey
- Building footprint

### **Findings:**

- Model population totals and age and set breakdowns with associated uncertainty measures within gird cells
