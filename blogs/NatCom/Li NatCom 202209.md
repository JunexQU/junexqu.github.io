## [Li, L., Zhang, Y., Zhou, T. et al. Mitigation of China’s carbon neutrality to global warming. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-33047-9)

### **Objective:**
- Project the mitigation of China's carbon neutrality to global warming by comparing four pairs of simulations corresponding to four SSPs

### **Case:**
- China

### **Methodology:**
- NCAR community earth system model

### **Data Source**
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- Global mean surface temperature (GMST) averaged over 2081-2100 is projected to increase by 1.7 under low  GHG emission scenario, by 2.7 and 3.4 under two intermediate emission scenario
