## [Abdul-Manan, A.F.N., Gordillo Zavaleta, V., Agarwal, A.K. et al. Electrifying passenger road transport in India requires near-term electricity grid decarbonisation. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-29620-x)

### **Objective:**
- Model the life cycle GHG emissions of over 600 passenger road vehicles comprising two-wheelers and four wheelers available in India

### **Case:**
- India

### **Methodology:**
- Life cycle assessment

### **Data Source**
- Cars
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- An electricity 2W is substantially more energy-efficient than a traditional gasoline 2 W, carrying only a minor manufacturing GHG penalty
