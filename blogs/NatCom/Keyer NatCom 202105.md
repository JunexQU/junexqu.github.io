## [Keyßer, L.T., Lenzen, M. 1.5°C degrowth scenarios suggest the need for new mitigation pathways. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-22884-9)

### **Objective:**
- How degrowth scenarios perform compared with IPCC SR1.5 IAM scenario archetypes regarding key relative risk indicators for feasibility and sustainability

### **Case:**
- Global 

### **Methodology:**
- Scenario simulation

### **Data Source**
- IEA: Energy demand
- CDIAC, FAOSTAT: CO2 emission

### **Findings:**

- Degrowth pathways exhibit the lowest relative risks for feasibility and sustainability when compared with established IPCC SR1.5 pathways using socio-technological indicators
