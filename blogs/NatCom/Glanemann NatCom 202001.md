## [Glanemann, N., Willner, S.N. & Levermann, A. Paris Climate Agreement passes the cost-benefit test. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-019-13961-1)

### **Objective:**
- Examine whether the damages that follow from extrapolating the observed relation of economic growth and temperature 

### **Case:**
- Global grids

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: DICE(GAMS)
- Damage function

### **Data Source**
- SSP


### **Findings:**

- The 2C target as set by the Paris Climate Agreement gives the cost-benefit optimal pathway till the end of this century
