## [Strefler, J., Kriegler, E., Bauer, N. et al. Alternative carbon price trajectories can avoid excessive carbon removal. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-22211-2)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse the effects resulting from different shapes of carbon price pathways and the implications for designing economically reasonable climate policies in a cost-effectiveness framework

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: REMIND

### **Data Source**

### **Findings:**

- In a cost-effectiveness framework with an explicit carbon budget limit, the optimal carbon price pathways shows an exponential increase at the rate of the discount rate
