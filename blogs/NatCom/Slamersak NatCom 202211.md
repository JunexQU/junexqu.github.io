## [Slameršak, A., Kallis, G. & O’Neill, D.W. Energy requirements and carbon emissions for a low-carbon energy transition. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-33976-5)

### **Objective:**
- Estimate how much energy would be required, and how much carbon would likely emitted, to construct, operate, and maintain the global energy system during a low-carbon energy transition

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Energy return on investment model
- Emission

### **Data Source**
- SSP
- IEA

### **Findings:**

- Energy systems emission are substantial
