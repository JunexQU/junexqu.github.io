## [Bistline, J.E.T., Blanford, G., Grant, J. et al. Economy-wide evaluation of CO2 and air quality impacts of electrification in the United States. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-33902-9)

### **Objective:**
- Evaluate CO2 and air quality co-benefits of electrification scenarios by linking a detailed energy systems model and a full-form photochemical air quality model

### **Case:**
- US

### **Methodology:**
- Life cycle assessment

### **Data Source**
- Energy
- Emission
- Electricity

### **Findings:**

- Electricity represents about 20% of final energy, which grows in the high electrification scenario to 31% in 2035
