## [Zhuo, Z., Du, E., Zhang, N. et al. Cost increase in the electricity supply to achieve carbon neutrality in China. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-30747-0)

### **Objective:**
- Establish a power system planning model with a high RE capacity share to evaluate the impacts on the electricity supply cost associated with China;s transition to carbon neutrality

### **Case:**
- Grid, China

### **Methodology:**
- Bilateral trade input-output approach

### **Data Source**
- FAOSTAT

### **Findings:**

- Global trade-adjusted agricultural emission (TAE) in total absolute terms increased during 1987-2015
