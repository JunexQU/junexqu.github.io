- Develop a transport energy model by integrating a transport model and an energy system model to project the future energy demand and emissions

- Provinces in China

### **Methodology:**
- Avoid-Shift-Improve framework
- IAM: AIM/Enduse

### **Data Source**

- Policy interventions would lead to different reduction potentials in the cumulative emissions
