## [Humpenöder, F., Popp, A., Schleussner, CF. et al. Overcoming global inequality is critical for land-based mitigation in line with the Paris Agreement. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-35114-7)

### **Objective:**
- Present a model-based quantification of two contrasting future scenarios for the GHG emissions from land sector (AFOLU)

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: MAgPIE-Land use

### **Data Source**
- Land use
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- In global-inequality scenario, global net AFOLU GHG emissions remain positive until 2100, which is considerably higher than 1.5 targets
