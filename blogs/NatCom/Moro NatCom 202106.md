## [Moro, E., Calacci, D., Dong, X. et al. Mobility patterns are associated with experienced income segregation in large US cities. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-24899-8)

### **Objective:**
- A more through understanding of behavior and mobility beyond residence, including individual motivation for visiting different places and encountering other groups of people

### **Case:**
- 11 US CBSAs

### **Methodology:**
- Exploration and preferential return model
- Income segregation index
- OLS

### **Data Source**
- Mobile phone: Cuebiq
- ACS

### **Findings:**

- Most areas in cities are home to both highly integrated and highly segregated places
