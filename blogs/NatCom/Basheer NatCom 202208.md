## [Basheer, M., Nechifor, V., Calzadilla, A. et al. Balancing national economic policy outcomes for sustainable development. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32415-9)

### **Objective:**
- Introduce a framework for designing and screening efficient natioanl economic policy portfolios that are aligned with the SDGs

### **Case:**
- Africa

### **Methodology:**
- CGE + AI-driven multiobjective evolutionary algorithm

### **Data Source**
- SSP
- SAM, GTAP

### **Findings:**

- Integrate policy strategies can help achieve sustainable development while balancing adverse economic, social and political impacts of reforms
