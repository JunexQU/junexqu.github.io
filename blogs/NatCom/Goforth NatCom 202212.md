## [Goforth, T., Nock, D. Air pollution disparities and equality assessments of US national decarbonization strategies. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-35098-4)

### **Objective:**
- Create a forward-looking analysis framework for assessing how decarbonization benefits will be shared across different demographic groups by trying a capacity expansion model with a local equality analysis

### **Case:**
- Census tract, US

### **Methodology:**
- Regional energy deployment system
- InMAP

### **Data Source**
- Electricity
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- No decarbonization scenario reaches operating emission distributional equality until they meet their mandate year of 2035 or 2050
