## [Liu, Y., Du, M., Cui, Q. et al. Contrasting suitability and ambition in regional carbon mitigation. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-31729-y)

### **Objective:**
- Contrast the average reduction cost of carbon against the potential benefit, valued to be equivalent to the scocial cost of carbon (SCC)

### **Case:**
- 27 global countries and regions

### **Methodology:**
- IAMs: AIM/CGE, GCAM, REMIND-MAGPIE, IMAGE, and WITCH-GLOBIOM
- RCC: GTAP-E model

### **Data Source**
- GTAP
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- Higher mitigation targets tend to have higher global average reduction cost of carbon (RCC)
