## [Qiu, Y., Lamers, P., Daioglou, V. et al. Environmental trade-offs of direct air capture technologies in climate change mitigation toward 2100](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-31146-1)

### **Objective:**
- Calculate a prospective life-cycle assessment (LCA) of direct air carbon capture and storage (DACCS) under climate change mitigation scenarios

### **Case:**
- US

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: IMAGE

### **Data Source**
- SSP

### **Findings:**

- A rapid decarbonization of  the power and energy demand sectors can increase the net sequestration efficiency of DACCS
