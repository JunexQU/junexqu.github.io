## [Verschuur, J., Koks, E.E. & Hall, J.W. Ports’ criticality in international trade and global supply-chains. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32070-0)

### **Objective:**
- Present a new modelling framework that provides a comprehensive understanding of the different dimensions of the criticality of ports for domestic and global economies

### **Case:**
- Global countries, ports

### **Methodology:**
- OxMarTrans Model: route choice
- Modal split

### **Data Source**
- BACI trade database
- Modal: UN Comtrade
- MRIO: EORA
- [PortInteruption](https://github.com/jasperverschuur/Port_Disruption_database)

### **Findings:**

- The share of maritime in global trade is about 75% in terms of weight and 50% in terms of value in 2015; differences exist across sectors
