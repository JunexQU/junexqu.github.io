## [Li, X., Gallagher, K.P. Assessing the climate change exposure of foreign direct investment. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-28975-5)

### **Objective:**
- Examine the physical climate risks of FDI

### **Case:**x
- Global firms

### **Methodology:**
- Fixed effects regression

### **Data Source**
- Firm-industry-host-country data
- FDI

### **Findings:**

- FDI is exposed to lower physical climate risks compared with local firms
