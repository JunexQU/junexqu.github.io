## [Shi, Q., Zheng, B., Zheng, Y. et al. Co-benefits of CO2 emission reduction from China’s clean air actions between 2013-2020. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32656-8)

### **Objective:**
- Quantify the CO2 emission reduction co-benefits of China's clean air action

### **Case:**
- Province, China

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: MEIC model

### **Data Source**
- Emission
- Energy

### **Findings:**

- Emissions of SO2 NOx and PM2.5 are estiamted to decline by 69%, 28% and 44%, respectively between 2013 and 2020
