## [Zhu, S., Mac Kinnon, M., Carlos-Carlos, A. et al. Decarbonization will lead to more equitable air quality in California. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-33295-9)

### **Objective:**
- Consider two scenarios to provide insight into the impacts of technology innovation, policy and otehr drivers that will shape the evolution of energy sectors

### **Case:**
- 4 km * 4km, California

### **Methodology:**
- Lorenz-curve based method

### **Data Source**
- Emission
- Building

### **Findings:**

- A lower incremental cost for the building electrification scenario relative to the trunk electrification scenario
- By 2050, GHG emissions will be reduced by 88% for transportation, 78% for industry and agriculture and 90% for buildings
