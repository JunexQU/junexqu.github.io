## [van Soest, H.L., den Elzen, M.G.J. & van Vuuren, D.P. Net-zero emission targets for major emitting countries consistent with the Paris Agreement. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-22294-x)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse national neutrality targets for major emission countries by using a set of scenarios by IAMs

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: AIM, IMAGE, MESSAGE-GLOBIOM, POLES, REMIND-MAGPIE, WITCH

### **Data Source**
- Scenario protocal

### **Findings:**

- For the median of the 2 scenarios, GHG emissions are projected to reach net zero earlier than the global level in Brazil, Japan, Russia and the US, but larger than global average in Canada, China, EU, India and Turkey
