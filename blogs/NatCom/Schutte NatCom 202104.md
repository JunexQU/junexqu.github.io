## [Schutte, S., Vestby, J., Carling, J. et al. Climatic conditions are weak predictors of asylum migration. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-22255-4)

### **Objective:**
- Quantify the relationship between climate change and migration

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Random forest
- Two-fixed effects regression 
- lagged

### **Data Source**
- Climate
- Economy
- Violence

### **Findings:**

- Conditions in countries of origin, do not provide a complete view of the determinants of asylum migration
