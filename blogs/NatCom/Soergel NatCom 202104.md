## [Soergel, B., Kriegler, E., Bodirsky, B.L. et al. Combining ambitious climate policies with efforts to eradicate poverty. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-22315-9)

### **Objective:**
- Compute the resulting changes in the income distribution and the effects on national, regional and global poverty rates

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: REMIND-MAgPIE
- Loss function
- Decomposition
- OLS

### **Data Source**
- SSP

### **Findings:**

- Mitigation policies without associated redistribution policies would lead to and increase in poverty compared to the baseline trend
