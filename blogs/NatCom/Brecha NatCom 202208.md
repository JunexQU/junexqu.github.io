### **Objective:**
- Present a transparent temperature assessment of institutional decarbonization scenarios 

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Constant quantile extension
- Quantile rolling windows
- IAM: MAGICC6, FaIR

### **Data Source**
- SSP

### **Findings:**

- Most institutional pathways cannot achieve the Paris Agreement target since the continued reliance on fossil fuels
