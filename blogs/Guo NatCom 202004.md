## [Guo, J., Gu, F., Liu, Y. et al. Assessing the impact of ETS trading profit on emission abatements based on firm-level transactions. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-020-15996-1)

### **Objective:**
- Examine the effectiveness of the market incentives that aims at stimulating abatements for trading

### **Case:**
- EU

### **Methodology:**
- Quantile regression

### **Data Source**
- Firm transaction data: EUTL

### **Findings:**

- Abatements for trading can be well-realized as trading profits
