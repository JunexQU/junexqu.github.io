## [Bergero, C., Gosnell, G., Gielen, D. et al. Pathways to net-zero emissions from aviation. Nat Sustain 6, 404–414 (2023)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41893-022-01046-9)

### **Objective:**
- Assess nine possible pathways to achieve net-zero direct emissions from avaition

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Carbon emission: $F = D(\frac{E}{D})(\frac{F}{E})= Def$
- Kaya formula
- Scenario: energy switching

### **Data Source: partial open**
- Carbon emission: IEA and CMP
- Passenger demand: ICAO
- Freight demand: world bank

### **Findings:**

- Without ambitious reductions in air transport demand and improvements in aircraft energy efficiency, decarbonmizing aviation will require important quantities of drop-in sustainable aviation fuels (SAF) 
- net-zero emission relies on the carbon dioxide removals (CDR)
