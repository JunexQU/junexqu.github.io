## [Fan, W., Schor, J.B., Kelly, O. et al. Work time reduction via a 4-day workweek finds improvements in workers' well-being. Nat Hum Behav](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41562-025-02259-6)

### **Objective:**
- Study how a workplace intervention that reduces the work-week from 5 to 4 days without corresponding reductions in pay effects worker's well-being

### **Case:**
- Firms across 4 countries

### **Methodology:**
- OLS
- Mixed linear regression
- Mediation

### **Data Source**
- Survey

### **Findings:**

- Work time reduction is associated with improvements in employee well-being
