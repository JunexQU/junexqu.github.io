## [Moore, F.C., Lacasse, K., Mach, K.J. et al. Determinants of emissions pathways in the coupled climate–social system. Nature 603, 103–111 (2022)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-022-04423-8)

### **Objective:**
- Model the drivers of potential emissions scenarios over the twenty-first century and, in doing so, shed light on how both climate policy and emissions arise from more fundamental socio-politico-technical forces and the key parameters governing these dynamics

### **Case:**
- OECD, Swedish

### **Methodology:**
- Monte Carlo
- k-means

### **Data Source**
- Parameter: reveiw
- Public opinion on climate change: OECD
- Carbon price: worldbank

### **Findings:**

- Identified emissions trajectories, even the aggressive action scenario, fail to meet the more ambitious Paris Agreement target
