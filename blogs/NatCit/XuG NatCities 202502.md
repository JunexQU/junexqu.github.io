## [Xu, G., Zhu, M., Chen, B. et al. Underlying rules of evolutionary urban systems in Africa. Nat Cities](https://doi.org/10.1038/s44284-025-00208-y)

### **Objective:**
- Uncover how the African urban systems evolved over a long-term period three three underlying urban rules

### **Case:**
- Africa cities

### **Methodology:**
- Three scaling laws

### **Data Source**
- Africa urban data
- Built up area
- Population

### **Findings:**

- More urbanized countries tend to have a more centralized urban system, where larger cities dominate
