- How planning decisions affect travellers' exposure to disamenities
- Two mechanisms are discussed: market-based processes that result in discriminatory outcomes; interprets these outcomes as the result of deliberate discrimination by policymakers

### **Case:**
- France

### **Methodology:**
- Probit regression
