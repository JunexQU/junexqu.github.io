## [Salazar-Miranda, A., Talen, E. An AI-based analysis of zoning reforms in US cities. Nat Cities](https://doi.org/10.1038/s44284-025-00214-0)

### **Objective:**
- Employ natural language propcessing to assess form-based codes (FBC) related zoning in more than 2000 census-designated places across the USA

### **Case:**
- US 

### **Methodology:**
- NLP, Regression

### **Data Source**
- Zoning codes: documents
- Census
- Employer-household OD
- Land use
- OSM

### **Findings:**

- FBC qualities corresponded to a discernible and distinct type of zoning code, distinguishable from othertypes of codes
