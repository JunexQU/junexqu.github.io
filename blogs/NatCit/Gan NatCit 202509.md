## [Gan, T., Dighe, T. & Porfiri, M. Trade-off between diversity and provision of specialized healthcare in US cities. Nat Cities](https://doi.org/10.1038/s44284-025-00326-7)

### **Objective:**
- Perform urban scaling analysis to elucidate how the prevalence of each specialty varies with the population size and density of a city

### **Case:**
- US cites
### **Methodology:**
- Scaling law

### **Data Source**
- Doctor and clinic individual data

### **Findings:**

- 94.67% of medical specialties scale sublinearly and the remaining 5.33% scale linearly with population size
