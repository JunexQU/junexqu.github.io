## [Kaufmann, D., Wicki, M., Wittwer, S. et al. Democratic discrepancies in urban sustainable development. Nat Sustain](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41893-024-01425-4)

### **Objective:**
- Examine residents' preferences with respect urban sustainable development policy issue and to compare these preferences with existing USD policy plans

### **Case:**
- 8 EU cities

### **Methodology:**
- Life-cycle simulation

### **Data Source**
- Survey

### **Findings:**

- Basic human need is deemed most important
