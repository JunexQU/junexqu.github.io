## [Chan, S., Hale, T., Deneault, A. et al. Assessing the effectiveness of orchestrated climate action from five years of summits. Nat. Clim. Chang. 12, 628–633 (2022)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-022-01405-6)

### **Objective:**
- Understanding the growing numbers of climate initiatives associated with summits and mobilization processes using a dataset that tracks their output performance

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- t-test
- OLS

### **Data Source**

- Climate coorperative initiatives database: 276 transnational climate initiatives during 2017-2020

### **Findings:**

- Average output is not increaseing comparing initiatives under successive orchestration efforts
