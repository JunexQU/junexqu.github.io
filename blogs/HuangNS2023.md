## [Huang, X., Srikrishnan, V., Lamontagne, J. et al. Effects of global climate mitigation on regional air quality and health. Nat Sustain 6, 1054–1066 (2023)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41893-023-01133-5)

### **Objective:**
- How will global climate mitigation affect regional air quality and health in the twenty-first century

### **Case:**
- Global region (32)

### **Methodology:**
- Globan change analysis model ([GCAM](https://jgcri.github.io/gcam-doc/index.html))
- Reduced form air pollution model: [TM5-FASST](http://tm5-fasst.jrc.ec.europa.eu/)
- Country level health impact assessment module


### **Data Source: partial open**
- [Open Data](https://zenodo.org/records/6975580)

### **Findings:**

- Carbon price reduces the global average temperature by 0.1 $^\circ C$
- Pricing carbon improves global air quality and reduces average PM2.5 attributeable death rates
