## [Testi, I., Wang, A., Paul, S. et al. Big mobility data reveals hyperlocal air pollution exposure disparities in the Bronx, New York. Nat Cities](https://doi.org/10.1038/s44284-024-00093-x)

### **Objective:**
- Assess air pollution exposure and its disparities in the urban context by leveraging big mobility data and street level air quality measurements

### **Case:**
- New York City

### **Methodology:**
- Daily exposure index
- Absolute exposure index
- Theil index

### **Data Source**
- GPS records
- ACS
- Environmental sensing platform

### **Findings:**

- Building area is negatively correlated with PM2.5 pollution
