## [Schöngart, S., Nicholls, Z., Hoffmann, R. et al. High-income groups disproportionately contribute to climate extremes worldwide. Nat. Clim. Chang](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-025-02325-x)

### **Objective:**
- Generate counterfactual emission pathways by subtracting the 1990-2019 emissions of specific emitter groups 

### **Case:**
- World Countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: MAGICC, MESMER-M-TP

### **Data Source**
- Heat

### **Findings:**

- 65% of the increase is attributable to the global top 10% and 20%
