## [Wang, S., Zhan, W., Zhou, B. et al. Dual impact of global urban overheating on mortality. Nat. Clim. Chang](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-025-02303-3)

### **Objective:**
- Establish robust M-T relationships and quantified the effectiveness of prevalent heat mitigation measures across more than 3,000 cities worldwide  

### **Case:**
- 3,280 cities around the world

### **Methodology:**
- Machine learning

### **Data Source**
- Urban surface air temperature (SAT)
- Mortality
- Vegetation index
- CMIP6

### **Findings:**

- Reduction in cold-related mortality induced by the UHI effects is 4.4 times greater than the increase in heat-related mortality
