## [Vivier, L., Mastrucci, A. & van Ruijven, B. Meeting climate target with realistic demand-side policies in the residential sector. Nat. Clim. Chang](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-025-02348-4)

### **Objective:**
- Conduct a comprehensive quantitative assessment of 384 demand-side policy combinations to support residential space-heating decarbonization in the EU-27 by mid-century

### **Case:**
- EU-27

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: MESSAGEix-GLOBIOM

### **Data Source**
- Technology and heat

### **Findings:**

- Cost reductions and heat-pump subsidies are essential to achieve sustainable development
