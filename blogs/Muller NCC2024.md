## [Müller-Casseres, E., Leblanc, F., van den Berg, M. et al. International shipping in a world below 2 °C. Nat. Clim. Chang](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41558-024-01997-1)

### **Objective:**
- Explore possible futures of international shipping in terms of energy carriers and CO2 emissions and IAM framework.

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- IAM
  - COFFEE: land use
  - IMACLIM-R: CGE
  - IMAGE: energy
  - PROMETHEUS: energy
  - TIAM-UCL: energy
  - WITCH: Syn

### **Data Source**

- [Data](https://zenodo.org/records/10836455)
