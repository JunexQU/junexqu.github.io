## [Yang, Y., Qu, S., Cai, B. et al. Mapping global carbon footprint in China. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-020-15883-9)

### **Objective:**
- Link carbon emission within mainland China to final consumers worldwide through both international and interprovincial trade

### **Case:**
- China

### **Methodology:**
- MRIO

### **Data Source**
- Industrial classifications and carbon emission: CHRED
- EORA and MRIO in China

### **Findings:**

- The total carbon footprint driven by foreign final consumption accounts for 14.6% of the total industrial-related CO2 emissions
