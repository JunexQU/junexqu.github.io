## [Jenn, A. Emissions benefits of electric vehicles in Uber and Lyft ride-hailing services. Nat Energy 5, 520–525 (2020)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41560-020-0632-7)

### **Objective:**
- Measure the impact of combining shared mobility services, particularly ride-hailing services, with vehicle electrification 

### **Case:**
- California

### **Methodology:**
- Emission indicator

### **Data Source**

- Survey: CHTS and PH-EV panel survey
- Charging: charging network
- Ride-hailing: Uber and Lyft

### **Findings:**

- Gasoline vehicles tend to be driven slightly more than their electric vehicle
