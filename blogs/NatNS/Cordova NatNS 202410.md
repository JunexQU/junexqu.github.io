## [Cordova, T., Kissinger, M., Teschner, N. et al. Effects of demographic and technological trends on the mitigation of Israel’s environmental footprint. Nat Sustain](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41893-024-01440-5)

### **Objective:**
- Explore a country's capacity for environmental mitigation, examining how various factors combined can potentially achieve desired goals such as net-zero emissions by 2050

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Scenario prediction tool

### **Data Source**
- energy, carbon, population

### **Findings:**

- Electricity production is the main source of emissions, followed by transportation, food, construction, water and fuel for heat
