## [Yap, W., Wu, A.N., Miller, C. et al. Revealing building operating carbon dynamics for multiple cities. Nat Sustain](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41893-025-01615-8)

### **Objective:**
- Reveal how planning history, urban form and economic activity interact to shape heterogeneous emissions patterns across cities

### **Case:**
- 5 Metropolitans

### **Methodology:**
- Graph deep learning

### **Data Source**
- Buildings.
- Carbon emission

### **Findings:**

- Higher population density, larger building footprints and greater building volumes are associated with lower emissions per unit of built-up area
