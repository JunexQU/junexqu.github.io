## [A. Ramani, J. Alcedo, N. Bloom, (2024) How working from home reshapes cities, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci.](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2408930121)

### **Objective:**
- Assess the association between WFH and shifts in urban structure across a large sample of countries

### **Case:**
- 118 global cities

### **Methodology:**
- OLS

### **Data Source**
- Mastercard network of in-person transactions
- Migration flow: USPS
- Real estate: US
- Commuting: GPS card
- Survey: global survey of working arrangements

### **Findings:**

- Suburbs spending grow 15 pp more relative to their city centers
