- Study how the network structure of production affects an economy's long-term growth

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Leontief matrix
- Multiplier
- Production equilibrium

### **Data Source**

- WIOD

### **Findings:**

- Production network plays a major role in the long-term evolution of economies
