## [Barrage, Lint, and William Nordhaus. Policies, projections, and the social cost of carbon Results from the DICE-2023 model. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 121.13 (2024)](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2312030121)

### **Objective:**
- Calculate social cost of carbon

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Dynamic integrated model of climate and the economy: (DICE model)
  - Social welfare function
  - Production function
  - Quadratic function for demage
  - Abatement function
  - Carbon function and climate equations

### **Data Source**

- In model

### **Findings:**

- Current policy baseline implies increasing emissions in the coming decades
