## [Barrington-Leigh, Christopher, and Adam Millard-Ball. Global trends toward urban street-network sprawl. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 117.4 (2020)](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1905232116)

### **Objective:**
- How patterns of street-network sprawl are changing among and within countries, worldwide

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Index and spatial analysis

### **Data Source**
- GHSL and the Atlas of Urban Expansion

### **Findings:**

- A striking global trend toward increased street-network sprawl
- Sprawl in Latin American, Asia and EU rises monotonically
