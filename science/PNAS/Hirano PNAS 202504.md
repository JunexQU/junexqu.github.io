## [T. Hirano,& A.A. Toda,  Unbalanced growth and land overvaluation, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2423295122)

### **Objective:**
- Study the long-run behaviour of land prices in modern economies where the importance of land as as factor of production diminishes

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Malthusian

### **Data Source**
- Land
- Employment

### **Findings:**
- Land overevaluation emerges along the long-run transition from the Malthusian agricultural economy to the modern knowledge and service-based economy
