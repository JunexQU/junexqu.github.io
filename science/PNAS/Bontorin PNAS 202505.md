## [S. Bontorin,S. Centellegher,R. Gallotti,L. Pappalardo,B. Lepri,& M. Luca,  Mixing individual and collective behaviors to predict out-of-routine mobility, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2414848122)

### **Objective:**
- Introduce an approach for next-location prediction that dynamically integrates individual and collective mobility behaviours, leveraging the expected predictability of an individual's movement

### **Case:**
- 5 Cities in the US

### **Methodology:**
- Deep learning
- Markov chain

### **Data Source**
- GPS location: Filter users with fewer than two trajectories and remove the top 95th percentile of the most represented users
