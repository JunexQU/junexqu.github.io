## [P. Zhao,H. Wang,W.A.V. Clark,Y. Feng,Q. Liu,& Y. Cui,  ICT usage increases workforce geographical diversity, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2426901122)

### **Objective:**
- Investigate the effects of ICT on the connectedness of geographical spaces and labour mobility patterns

### **Case:**
- China

### **Methodology:**
- Geographical diversity index (GDI)
- Causal: PVAR, time series
- Robustness: subsample, COVID, IV, city size

### **Data Source**
- Mobile phone

### **Findings:**
- Large cities and male workers benefit more from ICT usage than small cities and female workers
