### **Objective:**
- Provides the most comprehensive assessment to date of social cost of CO2, including how elements of model structure shape the SCC

### **Case:**
- Global literature

### **Methodology:**
- literature
- survey

### **Data Source**
- Survey data

### **Findings:**

- Reweighting the literature to partly address the omissions in SCC more than doubles the SCC to $283.
