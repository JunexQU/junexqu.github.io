## [I. Lopez-Gomez,Z.Y. Wan,L. Zepeda-Núñez,T. Schneider,J. Anderson,& F. Sha,  Dynamical-generative downscaling of climate model ensembles, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci]( https://10.1073/pnas.2420288122)

### **Objective:**
- Demonstrate how probabilistic diffusion models trained on data from a single ESM can be used to downscale multimodal climate projections, relying on the proven generalization abilities of physical RCMs

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Probability diffusion model
- WRF
- CMIP6

### **Data Source**
- 46-km climate projection: WUS-D3
