## [C. Li,M. Li,L. Zhang,Q. Li,H. Zheng,& M.W. Feldman,  Energy-poverty-inequality SDGs: A large-scale household analysis and forecasting in China, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2408167121)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse the potential of clean cooking fuel transition in driving SDGs 1, 7 and 10

### **Case:**
- China

### **Methodology:**
- Regression
- Robust
  - introduce control
  - IV
  - endogeneity bias
  - Rubin causal model 
  - Change definition
- Moderated regression

### **Data Source**
- Survey: CFPS
- CHARLS
- CRECS

### **Findings:**

- The prevalence rate of clean cooking fuels rose from 60% in 2012 to 72% in 2018
