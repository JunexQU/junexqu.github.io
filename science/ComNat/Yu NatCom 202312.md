## [Yu, B., Zhao, Z., Wei, YM. et al. Approaching national climate targets in China considering the challenge of regional inequality. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-44122-0)

### **Objective:**
- Provide a carbon emissions mitigation strategy to fulfill the Chinese national climate pledges considering the national and provincial economies and equity

### **Case:**
- China's provinces

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: C3IAM/NET

### **Data Source**
- Energy use: iNEMS

### **Findings:**

- China is likely peak the carbon emissions before 2029 at no more than 12.2 billion tons of CO2
