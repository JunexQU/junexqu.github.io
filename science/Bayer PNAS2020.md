## [Bayer, Patrick, and Michaël Aklin. The European Union emissions trading system reduced CO2 emissions despite low prices. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 117.16 (2020)](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1918128117)

### **Objective:**
- Draw aon sectoral emissions data to answer whether carbon markets are effective in international settings 

### **Case:**
- EU

### **Methodology:**
- OLS
- Synthetic control

### **Data Source**
- EU sectoral emission data: EU ETS EUTL and National Emissions Report

### **Findings:**

- A decrease of CO2 emissions in ETS sectors of between 8.1 and 11.5%
