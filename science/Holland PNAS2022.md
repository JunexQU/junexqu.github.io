## [Holland, Stephen P., et al. Why marginal CO2 emissions are not decreasing for US electricity Estimates and implications for climate policy. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 119.8 (2022)](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2116632119)

### **Objective:**
- Estimate marginal CO2 emissions due to changes in systemwide electricity use

### **Case:**
- US

### **Methodology:**
- OLS

### **Data Source**
- Hourly emission: US EPA

### **Findings:**

- Marginal emissions have increased 7%
- Pushing coal to be more frequently and decreasing the efficiency increases the marginal emissions
