## [Reimann, L., Jones, B., Bieker, N. et al. Exploring spatial feedbacks between adaptation policies and internal migration patterns due to sea-level rise. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-38278-y)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse plausible internal migration patterns due to Sea-level Rise (SLR), specifically focusing on exploring the effects of coastal adaptation policies

### **Case:**
- Mediterranean region

### **Methodology:**
- CONCLUDE

### **Data Source**
- SSP
- GHSL

### **Findings:**
- SLR may result in over 20 million internal migrants by 2100 if no adaptation policies are pursued
