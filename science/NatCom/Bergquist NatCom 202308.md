## [Bergquist, P., Warshaw, C. How climate policy commitments influence energy systems and the economies of US states. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-40560-y)

### **Objective:**
- Use estimates of state climate policy to examine the environmental and economic consequences of state climate policies

### **Case:**
- US states

### **Methodology:**
- Climate policy index
- Bayesian factor analysis
- Time-series cross-sectional OLS

### **Data Source**
- Climate policy
- GDP
- Carbon emission

### **Findings:**
- More stringent state climate policy regimes are associated with meaningful reductions in CO2 emissions
