## [Zhang, C., Zhao, X., Sacchi, R. et al. Trade-off between critical metal requirement and transportation decarbonization in automotive electrification. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-37373-4)

### **Objective:**
- Quantify the spatiotemporal patterns of GHG emissions and critical metals requirement in electrifying the light-/heavy-duty passenger and freight road fleets under different penetration and energy transition scenarios

### **Case:**
- 48 global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: IMAGE

### **Data Source**
- Battery
- Cars
- Energy
- Carbon

### **Findings:**
- Electrifying transportation and shifting a renewable-based energy supply allows road transportation to reach a deep decarbonization
