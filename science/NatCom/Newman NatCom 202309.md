## [Newman, R., Noy, I. The global costs of extreme weather that are attributable to climate change. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-41888-1)

### **Objective:**
- Use the frequency approach to aggregate the global economic damage from extreme weather events attributable to anthropogenic climate change 

### **Case:**
- Global 

### **Methodology:**
- EEA
- IAM

### **Data Source**
- Extreme event
- Economic

### **Findings:**
- Anthropogenic climate change is responsible for a net $260.8 billion of economic damage, which is equivalent to 53% of the total damages recorded
