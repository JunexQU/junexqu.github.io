## [Tan, X., Huang, B., Batty, M. et al. The spatiotemporal scaling laws of urban population dynamics. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-58286-4)

### **Objective:**
- Uncover the temporal and spatial scaling patterns of urban population fluctuations

### **Case:**
- Several cities and metropolitans

### **Methodology:**
- Scaling law

### **Data Source**
- POI
- Population
- Mobility

### **Findings:**
- Population fluctuations in diverse urban systems across continents can be characterized by a set of scaling laws
