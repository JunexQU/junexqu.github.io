## [Wessel, J., Chowdhury, A.K., Wild, T. et al. Regional coordination can alleviate the cost burden of a low-carbon electricity system. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-64093-8)

### **Objective:**
- Assess the role of full regional coordination on electricity capacity expansion and deep decarbonization pathways among five countries usingGridPath model

### **Case:**
- Five countries in South American

### **Methodology:**
- GridPath model

### **Data Source**
- Electricity
- Wind nad solar PV, Hydropower

### **Findings:**

- Decarbonization is achievable with modest cost premiums which are further mitigated by international coordination
