## [Cenci, S., Burato, M., Rei, M. et al. The alignment of companies' sustainability behavior and emissions with global climate targets. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-43116-2)

### **Objective:**
- Develop a methodology to study corporate sustainability behaviour using natural language processes approaches 

### **Case:**
- Global firms

### **Methodology:**
- Topic analysis

### **Data Source**
- REFINITIV 
- sustainability report

### **Findings:**

- Each environmental SDG comprises multiple targets, but most of these targets are not related to reducing GHG emissions
