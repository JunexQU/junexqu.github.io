## [Yu, Q., He, B.Y., Ma, J. et al. California’s zero-emission vehicle adoption brings air quality benefits yet equity gaps persist. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-43309-9)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse the historical zero-emission vehicle (ZEV) adoption trend for disadvantaged communities (DAC) and non-DACs between 2015 and 2020

### **Case:**
- California

### **Methodology:**
- CalEnviroScreen 4.0
- Activity-based model: MATSim

### **Data Source**
- ZEV
- Emission

### **Findings:**

- ZEV ownership gaps between DACs and non-DACs still remain
