## [Boakes, E.H., Dalin, C., Etard, A. et al. Impacts of the global food system on terrestrial biodiversity from land use and climate change. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-49999-z)

### **Objective:**
- Examine the international production-based and consumption-based footprints of food-related commodities in 2011 in terms of land area, species richness, rarity-weighted species richness 

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Leontief

### **Data Source**
- MRIO: EXIOBASE 3

### **Findings:**
- Regions with high production-based footprint tend to have a high consumption-based footprint
