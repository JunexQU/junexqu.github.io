## [Brottrager, M., Crespo Cuaresma, J., Kniveton, D. et al. Natural resources modulate the nexus between environmental shocks and human mobility. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-37074-y)

### **Objective:**
- Examine the agricultural pathway of environmental risks to migration, highlighting the mechanism of deteriorating land resources due to drought

### **Case:**
- Continental Africa, grid

### **Methodology:**
- DID

### **Data Source**
- Grid population
- Drought

### **Findings:**
- Mobile populations would be expected to show increased migration both sub-nationally and internationally in times of environmental stress
