## [Ronco, M., Tárraga, J.M., Muñoz, J. et al. Exploring interactions between socioeconomic context and natural hazards on human population displacement. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-43809-8)

### **Objective:**
- Study human internal displacement induced by suddenonset hazards at a global scale with data-driven machine learning

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- ML: RF, GBM

### **Data Source**
- AWI, Precipitation, wind speed, kNDVI, population
- gHM, elevation, conflict fatalities, education, agricultural land
- Disaster: IDMC

### **Findings:**

- Asia is the most impacted continent regardless of the type of disaster, this is particularly evident in the most densely populated coastal regions
