## [Huo, J., Meng, J., Zheng, H. et al. Achieving decent living standards in emerging economies challenges national mitigation goals for CO2 emissions. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-42079-8)

### **Objective:**
- Assess the additional CO2 emissions that would arise in all emerging economie globally to achieve decent living standards (DLS) 

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- MRIO

### **Data Source**
- Emerging MRIO table
- Food: FAO
- ICT: UN
- Education, Water, Sanitation, Electricity: World Bank
- Mobility: ITF
- Health: GHED

### **Findings:**
- Achieving DLS in emerging economies need only generate 8.6 Gt of additional CO2 emissions and has no impact on global climate goals
