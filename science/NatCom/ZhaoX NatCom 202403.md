## [Zhao, X., Mignone, B.K., Wise, M.A. et al. Trade-offs in land-based carbon removal measures under 1.5 °C and 2 °C futures. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-46575-3)

### **Objective:**
- Explore how various mitigation policy choices affect the contribution of land-based CDR measures, their trade-offs, and the corresponding global market-meditated responses such as land use, bioenergy supply and agricultural price impacts

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- IAM: GCAM

### **Data Source**
- Land use
- Carbon emission

### **Findings:**
- Positive relationship between agricultural prices and removal effecttiveness of land-based mitigation
