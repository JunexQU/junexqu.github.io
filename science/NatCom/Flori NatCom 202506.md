## [Flori, A., Spelta, A. Carbon trade biases and the emerging mesoscale structure of the European Emissions Trading System network. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-59913-w)

### **Objective:**
- Graph-based methodologies to analyze EUAs trade patterns within the EU ETS network

### **Case:**
- EU Provinces

### **Methodology:**
- Gravity model (Poisson model)
- Optimization

### **Data Source**
- EU Trade data (ETS)

### **Findings:**

- Close units tend to trade more than distant units
