## [Liu, K., Wang, Q., Wang, M. et al. Global transportation infrastructure exposure to the change of precipitation in a warmer world. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-38203-3)

### **Objective:**
- Analyse future changes in the global exposure of roads and railways to precipitation in a warmer world using multi-model projections

### **Case:**
- Global countries, grid

### **Methodology:**
- Simulation

### **Data Source**
- CMIP: climate
- OSM

### **Findings:**
- 91.7-94.6% of the global land mass may experience decreasing return periods as a result of global warming
