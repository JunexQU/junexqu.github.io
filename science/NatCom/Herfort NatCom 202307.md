## [Herfort, B., Lautenbach, S., Porto de Albuquerque, J. et al. A spatio-temporal analysis investigating completeness and inequalities of global urban building data in OpenStreetMap. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-39698-6)

### **Objective:**
- Investigate building data completeness and inequality in OSM on the global scale for 13,189 urban centres around the world

### **Case:**
- Global urban centres

### **Methodology:**
- Random forest
- Generalized additive models
- K-means

### **Data Source**
- GHS-UCDB
- Building in OSM and Microsoft
- Geo-Wiki
- Population
- Road length
- Waterbody
- Fossil fuel consumption

### **Findings:**
- For 14% cities, OSM building footprint data exceed 80% completeness, while 69% cities do not reach 20% completeness
