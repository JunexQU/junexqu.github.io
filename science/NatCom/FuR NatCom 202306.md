## [Fu, R., Peng, K., Wang, P. et al. Tracing metal footprints via global renewable power value chains. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-39356-x)

### **Objective:**
- Develop a quantitative framework to gauge metal footprints (MFs) in global RPVCs by combining a MRIO with a value chain decomposition model

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Leontief matrix
- Structural decomposition analysis

### **Data Source**
- MRIO: EXIOBASE, GTAP, WIOD

### **Findings:**
- The MFs of the global renewable power demand increased by 97% from 2005 to 2015
