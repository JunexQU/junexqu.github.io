## [Xu, F., Huang, Q., Yue, H. et al. The challenge of population aging for mitigating deaths from PM2.5 air pollution in China. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-40908-4)

### **Objective:**
- Estimate the spatial and temporal patterns of deaths attributable PM2.5 air pollution (DAPP) based on the CMIP6 dataset, provincial-level disease mortality data and decomposition analysis

### **Case:**
- China's provinces

### **Methodology:**
- Comparable risk assessment framework: population attributable fraction
- GBD model
- Decomposition analysis

### **Data Source**
- Global PM2.5 assessment dataset
- CMIP6
- SSP

### **Findings:**
- Population-weighted PM2.5 concentration in China increased by 43.8% from 2000 to 2019
