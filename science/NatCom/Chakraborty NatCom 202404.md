## [Chakraborty, A., Reisch, T., Diem, C. et al. Inequality in economic shock exposures across the global firm-level supply network. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-46126-w)

### **Objective:**
- Focus on an accurate description of international economic shock propagation and compare the exposure to direct and indirect production losses with GDP growth

### **Case:**
- Global firms

### **Methodology:**
- Exposure indicator

### **Data Source**
- Supplier-customer data

### **Findings:**
- Economic exposure is strong between countries in the same geographic or economic region
