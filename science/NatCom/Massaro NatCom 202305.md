## [Massaro, E., Schifanella, R., Piccardo, M. et al. Spatially-optimized urban greening for reduction of population exposure to land surface temperature extremes. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-38596-1)

### **Objective:**
- Set a spatial model to predict the absolute value of the exposure of the population to land surface temperature (LST) extremes

### **Case:**
- Global 200 cities

### **Methodology:**
- Spatial lag model

### **Data Source**
- Green: NDVI
- LST: MOD11A1
- GHSL

### **Findings:**
- Urban vegetation plays a considerable role in decreasing the exposure of the urban population to LST extremes
