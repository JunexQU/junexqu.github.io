## [Fan, JL., Li, Z., Huang, X. et al. A net-zero emissions strategy for China's power sector using carbon-capture utilization and storage. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-41548-4)

### **Objective:**
- Construct a high-resolution integrated power system assessment model considering the hourly electricity supply-demand balance

### **Case:**
- China's provinces

### **Methodology:**
- Multi-objective optimization model

### **Data Source**
- Power supply

### **Findings:**
- The optimal high-renewable share power system configured cannot achieve net-zero emissions
