## [Ma, F., Wang, H., Tzachor, A. et al. The disparities and development trajectories of nations in achieving the sustainable development goals. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-56076-6)

### **Objective:**
- Examine whether countries have so-called orphaned SDG indicators in certain development areas and whether there are underlying patterns or rules governing these orphaned areas

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Specialization: Revealed comparative advantage
- Sustainability index
- Community: network
- Moving-window (Trend)

### **Data Source**
- SDG Report
