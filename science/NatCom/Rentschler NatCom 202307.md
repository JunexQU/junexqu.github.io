## [Rentschler, J., Leonova, N. Global air pollution exposure and poverty. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-39797-4)

### **Objective:**
- Explore the global prevalence of unsafe outdoor air pollution and the way it interacts with poverty

### **Case:**
- Global subnational units

### **Methodology:**
- Resampling

### **Data Source**
- PM2.5
- Poverty index: World Bank
- Boundaries: World Bank

### **Findings:**
- 7.3 million people face air pollution levels that are considered unsafe- they are exposed to annual average PM2.5 concentrations over 5 $\mu$g/m3, increasing mortaility rates by 4%
