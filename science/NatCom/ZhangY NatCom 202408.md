## [Zhang, Y., Jackson, C. & Krevor, S. The feasibility of reaching gigatonne scale CO2 storage by mid-century. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-51226-8)

### **Objective:**
- Generate projections of CO2 storage deployment geospatially around the world that are constrained by current or planned deployment, rates of growth, and the assessed geological resource base

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Logistic growth model

### **Data Source**
- AR6 scenario explorer: IIASA

### **Findings:**
- A maximum global storage rate of 16 GtCO2 per year by 2050, but this is contingent on the US contributing 60% of the total
