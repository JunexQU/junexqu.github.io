## [Zhao, P., Wang, H., Liu, Q. et al. Unravelling the spatial directionality of urban mobility. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-48909-7)

### **Objective:**
- Quantify the spatial directionality of human mobility, employing vector conputation that integrates both the magnitude and direction of mobility flows

### **Case:**
- 50 Cities in China

### **Methodology:**
- Two indicators

### **Data Source**
- Two-month mobile phone data

### **Findings:**
- Residents in monocentric cities face increasing commuting distances as city expand, in contrast to the consistent commuting patterns observed in polycentric cities
