## [Q., Wu, C., Chen, F. et al. Intranational synergies and trade-offs reveal common and differentiated priorities of sustainable development goals in China. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-46491-6)

### **Objective:**
- Analyze the synergies and trade-offs among the 17 SDGs in China at the national, provincial and regional levels

### **Case:**
- China, province

### **Methodology:**
- SDG indicator
- Absolute value of the correlation coefficient
- Network analysis

### **Data Source**
- Statistics

### **Findings:**
- At the national level, 1023 out of 5151 indicator pairs showed synergies and 374 pairs showed trade-offs
