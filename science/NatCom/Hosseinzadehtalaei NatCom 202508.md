## [Hosseinzadehtalaei, P., Hamdi, R., Moradkhani, H. et al. Inequality in human exposure to future climate extremes. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-025-63385-3)

### **Objective:**
- Explore the disparities in human exposure to projected floods, heatwaves, droughts and compound hot-dry events between low- and high-income countries

### **Case:**
- Global countries

### **Methodology:**
- Project future extreme risks via probability modelling
- Exposure index

### **Data Source**
- CMIP6 data of drought, heatwaves and hot-dry event
- GDP from World Bank

### **Findings:**
- The disparity in exposure to extreme events is driven more by population growth than climate change.
