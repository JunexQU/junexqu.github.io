## [Yang, L., Yang, Y., Shen, Y. et al. Urban development pattern’s influence on extreme rainfall occurrences. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-48533-5)

### **Objective:**
- Reveal contrasting urban development patterns during 2003-2018

### **Case:**
- Global cities, grid

### **Methodology:**
- K-means
- Weather research and forecasting model

### **Data Source**
- Global Artificial Impervious Areas
- Elevation: SRTM30+
- Railfall

### **Findings:**
- Expansions in urban coverage overall contribtues to increased frequencies of extreme railfall
