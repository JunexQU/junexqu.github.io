## [Han, D., Yu, D. & Qiu, J. Assessing coupling interactions in a safe and just operating space for regional sustainability. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-37073-z)

### **Objective:**
- Introduce an innovative coupling coordination degree (CDD) to the safe and just operating space (SJOS) analytical framework 

### **Case:**
- China's province

### **Methodology:**
- Mann-Kendall method: Long-term changing trend
- Coupling degree indicator
- Regression tree

### **Data Source**
- CO2 Water, Land, Biogeochemical flow
- Human-being

### **Findings:**
- Significant regional disparities in sustainability performance relative to SJOS
