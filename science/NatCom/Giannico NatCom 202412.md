- Estimate the total adult mortality burdent that could be prevented in all Italian municipalities by greening residential areas up to the level of greenness currently achieved by the 25% of the population

### **Case:**
- Italy, Grid

### **Methodology:**
- Exposure indicator

### **Data Source**
