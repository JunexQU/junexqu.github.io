## [Brazzola, N., Meskaldji, A., Patt, A. et al. The role of direct air capture in achieving climate-neutral aviation. Nat Commun](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-55482-6)

### **Objective:**
- Offer a global perspective on direct air capture (DAC) deployment to achieve CO2 and climate neutrality in aviation

### **Case:**
- Global

### **Methodology:**
- Life-cycle assessment

### **Data Source**
- Fuel
- Air demand

### **Findings:**
- Achieving CO2 neutrality through DACCU increases flight ticket prices only slightly relative to the DACCS pathway and even relative to bau pathway
